How Does it Work?

Discover the seamless process of turning your ideas into reality with 3D CREATIVES. From design submission to receiving your stunning prints, our streamlined workflow ensures a hassle-free experience in just a few easy steps.

1. Submit Your Design:
Provide your 3D design file in formats such as STL, OBJ, or STEP, along with any specific instructions or requirements for the project.

2. Review and Quote:
Our team will carefully review your design, assessing its feasibility and printing requirements. You will receive a detailed quote, including pricing and estimated turnaround time.

3. Production and Printing:
Once you approve the quote, our skilled technicians will commence the 3D printing process using state-of-the-art equipment. Quality checks will be performed throughout the production phase.

4. Finishing and Delivery:
If desired, we can apply finishing touches such as sanding, painting, or surface treatment to enhance the final appearance and functionality of your 3D printed object. Your print will be carefully packaged and promptly delivered to your specified location.
